About Us

Welcome to the Earth & Canvas Club, where our creativity is fueled by the beauty of the natural world around us. We are a community of artists and nature lovers who believe that the best studio is the one with no walls. Our mission is simple: to connect with nature, with our art, and with each other!

Each week, we explore a new, beautiful location. Trust us, there are plenty of spots all around us! Members bring their favorite supplies, whether it's a sketchbook and pencils or a full watercolor set, to capture the scenery around them or create new and original art. It's a welcoming space for all skill levels. Whether you are a seasoned professional or a curiou beginner looking to try something new, you are welcome!

If you're searching for fresh inspiration, a reason to spend more time outdoors, and to find a supportive group of creative people, you've found your place. Join us to unwind, create, and see where your artistic adventure takes you!

What You Need to Know...

  • Who Can Join

    We welcome all artists ages 18 and up, regardless of experience. Whether you're a seasoned painter or just starting, you'll find a supportive community here.

  • Art Supplies

    You are encouraged to bring any art supplies you love to work with. For those traveling light, we always provide a selection of basic drawing supplies and paints for everyone to use.

  • Getting There

    Our meetups explore new scenic spots each week, so members are responsible for their own transportation. You can always find the location on our “Events” page.

  • Comfort Items

    To ensure a relaxing time, we highly recommend bringing a blanket or portable chair, a water bottle, and snacks. Dressing in layers is a great idea for Boulder's weather!
