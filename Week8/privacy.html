Privacy Statement

What Data We Collect

Our website is designed to be simple and focused on our Earth & Canvas Club community. We collect very limited data to improve your experience:

  • User Preferences: When you change the text size using the controls in the footer, we save your choice in your browser's "local storage". This is the only piece of personal data we store.
  • External Data: Our site uses Adobe Fonts to display our fonts. When you visit our site, your browser requests these fonts from Adobe's servers. This process may involve Adobe collecting technical data of yours, but we do not have access to that information.

Why We Collect It

We save your text size preference for one simple reason which is convenience. By remembering your choice, we can automatically apply it the next time you visit, ensuring the text is always as readable as you like it without you having to set it each time.

Data Storage and The Duration

Your text size preference is stored directly in your web browser using a feature called "localStorage". It is not sent to our servers. This data will automatically expire and be deleted from your browser 30 days after you last set it.

Your Control Over Your Data

You have full control over your data.

  • Clearing Data: You can delete your stored text size preference at any time by clicking the "Clear Preferences" button in the site's footer.
  • Opting Out: Since data is only stored when you interact with the text-size buttons, you can opt out of this data collection by not using the feature.
  • Browser Controls: You can also clear your browser's local storage through your browser's settings at any time.
